Rapido Product Teardown – Product & Growth Analysis

This project is a detailed product teardown of Rapido, where I analyze the platform from a product management and product analytics perspective.

The goal of this teardown is to understand how a ride-hailing platform works, identify improvement opportunities, and evaluate product decisions using structured product frameworks.

What this teardown covers

i)Product overview and value proposition

ii)Target users and user personas

iii)User journey analysis of the ride booking process

iv)Key product metrics such as acquisition, activation, retention, and engagement

v)Funnel analysis for the ride-booking flow

vi)Competitive comparison with Uber and Ola

vii)Identification of pain points in the current user experience

viii)Suggested product improvements and growth opportunities .

Frameworks and concepts used

i AARRR (Pirate Metrics)

ii Funnel analysis

iii Product metrics evaluation

iv User journey mapping

v Product improvement frameworks

Objective of this project

The purpose of this teardown is to strengthen my understanding of product thinking, product analytics, and business problem-solving, which are essential skills for Product Analyst, Business Analyst, and Associate Product Manager roles.
